城市介绍

延安

延安位于陕西省北部，地处黄河中游，是中国革命的圣地。毛泽东等老一辈革命家曾在这里生活战斗了十三个春秋，领导了抗日战争和解放战争，培育了延安精神，为中国革命作出了巨大的贡献。延安的革命旧址全国数量最多，分布最广，级别最高。延安是全国爱国主义、革命传统、延安精神的教育基地。延安有九座革命纪念馆，珍藏着中共中央和老一辈革命家在延安时期留下的大量重要物品，因此享有“中国革命博物馆城”的美誉。

Yan’an is located in the northern part of Shaanxi Province, in the middle reaches of the Yellow River, and is a holy place of the Chinese Revolution. Mao Zedong and other revolutionaries of the old generation lived and fought here for thirteen springs and autumns, led the War of Resistance against Japan and the War of Liberation, cultivated the spirit of Yan’an, and made significant contributions to the Chinese Revolution. Yan’an has the largest number of old revolutionary sites in the country, the widest distribution and the highest level. Yan’an is a national educational base for patriotism 爱国主义, revolutionary traditions and the spirit of Yan’an. There are nine revolutionary memorials in Yan’an, which hold a large number of important items left behind by the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China 中共中央 and the old generation of revolutionaries during the Yan’an period, and thus enjoy the reputation of being the “Museum City of the Chinese Revolution”.
