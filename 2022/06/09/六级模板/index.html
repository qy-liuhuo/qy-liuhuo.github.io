谚语

where there is a will,there is a way.

开头段落

引入话题

With regard to 现象, viewpoints vary substantially(非常).

With the continuous progress of xx, a tremendous number of youngers xxxx

This requires the joint efforts of all parties

其他人的观点

A tremendous number of people deem/contend that观点 while others seriously doubt this. They complain that….

自己的观点

Nevertheless，as far as i am concerned，
后面可以接

  • it’s no exaggeration to say that (毫不夸张的说)
  • no one disputes that (没人否认)
  • I am in line with the view that
  • this view does not hold water. (这个观点站不住脚)

引出原因

Reasons and concrete evidence to support my view point are as follows

主体段落

分点语句

First and foremost(最重要的)，

In the second place,

last but not least

其他高级句式

never can we disregard(忽视) the innumberable merites that xxx enjoys. (我们决不能忽视xx享有的优点) = never can we ignore the importance of xxx

The advantages of 事情 are numerous.

道理解释

To elucidate，(解释一下)

  • The major determinant lies in
  • The second noteworthy(值得注意的) reason is that

… is a contributing factor as well.

举例

This point is best illustrated by the case of…

… is a prime/classic example

For instance

小结

Thereby，it is utterly manifest（非常明显的）that ….

It reinforces my point that (再次强调了我xxx的观点)

结尾

To sum up/In a nutshell，from all the analysis above

considering all the factors above.

It is reasonable to reach the conclusion that…..

I take a firm belief that the future of xx will be brighter with our joint efforts.
